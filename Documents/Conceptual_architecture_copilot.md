CORE IDEA: A set of concentric, free-flowing liquid layers surrounding a central D-T plasma core. Each layer both receives and supplies energy, forming a closed-loop, self-stabilizing system.

LAYERS (inside → out)
1) Plasma Core
   - Fuel: Deuterium-Tritium
   - Function: Fusion reaction; primary source of energy (alpha + neutron + EM emissions).
   - Properties: Highly conductive plasma; generates synchrotron radiation and neutrons.

2) Molten Iron Inner Layer (Inner Dynamo)
   - Function: Base magnetic field generation (stable muscle field); first neutron moderator/shield; thermal sink to stay molten.
   - Role: Provides a strong, stable magnetic baseline and mechanical anchor for nested fields.

3) Pb-Li Outer Liquid Layer (Outer Dynamo / Field Creation Layer)
   - Material: Lead-Lithium eutectic conceptually (conductive liquid alloy).
   - Function: Resonant control, MHD-driven rotation, tritium breeding from neutrons, precision field fine-tuning.
   - Role: Converts neutron kinetic input into controlled rotational MHD energy to generate/adjust containment field.

4) Fast-flowing Lithium Curtain (Self-healing Window)
   - Function: Active, replaceable thermal/neutron shield immediately outside plasma boundary; lets EM (synchrotron) energy pass to metamaterial while absorbing heat and neutrons.
   - Role: Protects metamaterial/electronics from direct plasma exposure and is continuously renewed.

5) Structural Casing with Inductive Coil Array
   - Function: Houses harvesting coils (inductive), supports outer systems, collects field spillover to produce DC electricity.
   - Role: Converts containment field dynamics into usable electric power.

ENERGY FLOWS (summary)
- Alpha particles (~20%): trapped; provide self-heating of plasma (helps sustain burn).
- Neutrons (~80%): exit plasma; transfer kinetic energy into liquid layers (Pb-Li and iron); converted conceptually into directed fluid motion rather than being left as waste heat.
- Synchrotron/Bremsstrahlung (EM losses): harvested by metamaterial aperture; converted into precise electrical power for control and to drive MHD pumps/field tuning.
- Output power: harvested primarily via inductive coils around the outer casing (field spillover), not via steam turbines.
